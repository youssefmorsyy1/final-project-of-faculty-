Models & Methodology

Every model is trained offline (python main.py --train), evaluated with leakage-safe cross-validation (GroupKFold / StratifiedGroupKFold by match, plus a held-out 2022 season), and registered in PostgreSQL. The cards below are generated live from the model_registry table via /api/models; diagnostic figures are served from the trained artifacts.
