Match & Squad Composition
Match-Level Distributions

Goals-per-match follows an approximately Poisson shape, home sides outscore away sides on average, and total scoring is broadly stable across seasons — the basis for the expected-points model on the dashboard.

Player Demographics

Squad ages cluster around the late-20s, Spanish players dominate the Transfermarkt-linked pool, and the positional mix is led by centre-backs and central midfielders.

Competition Coverage

Domestic-league clubs carry the bulk of the per-team match volume, while the international tournaments contribute the broadest spread of distinct teams.

Per-Player Performance Signals
Key Metric Distributions

Attacking outputs (xG, xA, shots) are heavily right-skewed — most player-match rows contribute little, with rare high-volume games — whereas pass accuracy and minutes played are concentrated near their upper range.

Spearman Correlation Matrix

Shots and xG are the strongest correlates of goals; passing and carrying volume form a tight cluster, and defensive actions correlate among themselves — the structure the clustering and cohesion models exploit.

Metric Trends Across Seasons

Pass accuracy trends upward over time while pressing volume declines in the most recent seasons — a stylistic shift worth controlling for when comparing players across eras.

Injury Landscape
Injury Overview

Most absences are short, muscle injuries dominate the type breakdown, and recorded injuries grow with data coverage in recent years — the label base for the injury-risk model.

Injury Rate by Starting Position

Full-backs and wide defenders show the highest 30-day injury rates, giving the risk model a meaningful positional signal beyond raw workload.

Match-Day Weather
Weather Conditions Across Venues

Historical match-day weather from the Open-Meteo archive, joined to each fixture by stadium coordinates. Most matches are played in clear, mild conditions; the long tails (cold, heavy rain, high wind) are the cases an environmental model would have to lean on.
